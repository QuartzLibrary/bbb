This site shows GWAS scores for genomes from the 1000 Genomes Project. For each GWAS, you can simulate the effect of the top [n] edits on these scores. The highest impact edits are chosen for each individual, optionally limited to the [m] variants with the lowest p-values.
Note that the edits simulated here are not necessarily causal. They are of variants statistically associated with traits in GWAS, which may be spurious or due to correlation with true causal variants.
Also, the scores shown are not direct measures of the phenotype, but ~roughly the portion of variance in the phenotype explained by the genotypes. These are based on effect sizes from GWAS summary statistics and assume additivity (it's a pretty good assumption in this case).
